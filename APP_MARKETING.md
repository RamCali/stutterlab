# StutterLab — App Marketing Playbook

> "First-time founders are obsessed with product. Second-time founders are obsessed with distribution." — Justin Kan

## Core Principle

**Distribution > Features.** AI makes building easy, so competition is exploding. The single most important thing for app growth in 2026 is **channel-market fit** — choosing the right distribution channel and mastering it.

---

## Distribution Channels

### 1. ASO (App Store Optimization)

**The #1 element: Keyword research** — "it's like mining for gold."

- Research keywords: "stuttering app", "speech training", "fluency practice", "speech exercises", "stutter help"
- Tool: [asommobile.net](https://asommobile.net) — look up any app or competitor's keywords
- Find competitors ranking for relevant keywords, analyze their download volume
- Only 17% of all apps make $1,000/month — hitting $1K/mo is the first real milestone
- Run ASO updates regularly, then pair with promotional bursts (see Lifetime Free Hack below)

**Case studies:**
- App ranking #1 for a keyword → grew from 1-2K/mo to 8-10K/mo downloads
- Launched app to 150 organic downloads/day → $1,600 in 22 days, $42K first year
- Established app from $70K to $200K/mo revenue with ASO tweaks alone

### 2. Apple Search Ads (ASA)

**Best launch combo: ASO + ASA together.**

- Easy to set up — no MMP (mobile measurement partner) needed
- Tie ads to custom product pages
- Search intent = best intent — people searching for your category are already interested
- The more niche the keyword, the better the conversion
- If you have zero organic downloads, all attribution goes to ASA — easy to measure
- Case study: Apps built to $100K+ revenue and sold for 7 figures using only ASO + ASA

**StutterLab keywords to bid on:**
- stuttering app
- speech fluency training
- stutter practice
- speech exercises for stuttering
- fluency shaping
- DAF app
- speech therapy app

### 3. Social Media Ads (Meta / TikTok / Snapchat)

**Case study — Arya:** $1M ARR, 20K paying members using only Meta ads + no-code tools (Typeform quiz, Squarespace website, Airtable content).

**Best practices:**
- Start with a **mobile web funnel** — users check out on web (no App Store 30% cut)
- Use a quiz (Typeform) to qualify users before they reach the app
- **No free trial on web** — hard paywall or discounted first month (e.g., $4 first month, renews at full price)
- Web checkout lets you charge higher prices
- Great for testing channel-market fit and product-market fit quickly

**Why paid marketing first:**
1. Build brand quickly
2. Test messaging — see what resonates with real spend
3. Understand target demographic
4. Learn your CAC (customer acquisition cost)
5. Build the flywheel — paid teaches you faster than organic
6. "Paid ads are the hardest truth — spending money forces honest feedback about your product"

### 4. Content-First Strategy

**Old way:** Build app → hope people want it → struggle to get users.
**New way:** Test viral content → validate demand → build what's already proven.

**How to validate:**
1. Use **SpyTok** to find viral ideas in the stuttering/speech niche
2. Analyze hooks, formats, and themes that work
3. Create test content on a **new account**
4. Warm it up — engage with content your target audience watches (trains the algorithm)
5. **If crickets → don't build the feature.** If something goes viral → validated.

**TikTok research hack:**
- Go to Search tab → find niche → tap Filters → sort by like count + "this month"

**Content ideas for StutterLab:**
- Stuttering tips and fluency techniques (short-form)
- Before/after practice session clips
- "Day X of my stuttering program" series
- Myth-busting stuttering misconceptions
- SLP-backed credibility content

**Case study — Parallel Live:**
- Created viral content first → video blew up → then built the app
- Made $1.2M since inception
- Created an entirely new search category

### 5. Lifetime Free Hack (Promotional Burst)

Make app **free for 2 days** → promote on deal sites → revert to full price.

**How to run it:**
1. Best timing: right after an ASO update — download burst gives new keywords a push
2. Promote on [IndieAppSanta.com](https://indieappsanta.com) or Reddit subreddits for app deals
3. Add a ratings prompt (timed to a win moment) to maximize reviews during the burst
4. After 2 days, revert to full price

**Case study:**
- 106K downloads, 800 new ratings (200 US, 600 worldwide)
- $7K in sales within first month
- $90K+ in first year
- Download spike boosted keyword rankings, ratings push compounded it

## Pricing & Monetization

| Strategy | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| **Hard paywall on web** | No free trial — pay or leave. Filters for serious users |
| **Discounted first month** | e.g., $4 or $9.99 first month, renews at full price ($99/mo) |
| **Web checkout** | Avoids App Store 30% cut, allows higher prices |
| **"Go get that refund"** | Don't fear charging — your first sale matters more than free signups |
| **Refunds = free research** | Refund requests tell you exactly why people won't pay. Free users never will |
| **Lock features, charge** | Don't give features away free — you'll never learn what people value |
| **$1K/mo milestone** | Only 17% of apps hit this. First target to aim for |

### StutterLab Pricing Notes
- Current: Free trial (7-day, credit card required) + Premium ($99/mo or $999/yr)
- Web checkout via Stripe embedded — already avoids App Store cut
- Consider: discounted first month ($9.99) to reduce friction, renewing at $99/mo
- Consider: hard paywall test on web funnel (no free trial, just discounted first month)

## Onboarding & Retention

| Tip | Detail |
|-----|--------|
| **Ratings prompt timing** | After a **win moment** (user completes first practice session), NOT during onboarding or after paywall |
| **Fallback timing** | If no clear win moment, prompt on 2nd or 3rd app open — most users never return |
| **Web-to-app funnel** | Use a quiz to qualify users before they reach the app |
| **Warm up social accounts** | Before posting, engage with target audience content — trains the algorithm |

### StutterLab Win Moments for Ratings Prompt
1. After completing first AI conversation practice
2. After finishing Day 1 of the program
3. After earning first achievement/XP milestone
4. After completing first challenge

## Action Items

### Immediate (Week 1-2)
- [ ] Research ASO keywords using asommobile.net
- [ ] Set up Apple Search Ads campaign with niche stuttering keywords
- [ ] Implement ratings prompt after first completed practice session
- [ ] Create 5 short-form content pieces about stuttering tips for TikTok/Instagram

### Short-term (Month 1)
- [ ] A/B test discounted first month ($9.99) vs current 7-day free trial
- [ ] Build web-to-app quiz funnel (Typeform or custom) for paid social
- [ ] Run first Meta ads campaign targeting stuttering-related interests
- [ ] Test content on a fresh TikTok account in the stuttering/speech niche

### Medium-term (Month 2-3)
- [ ] Analyze CAC and LTV from paid channels
- [ ] Run Lifetime Free promotional burst after next ASO update
- [ ] Double down on the channel showing best ROI
- [ ] Create SLP-credibility content series for social media

### Long-term (Quarter 2+)
- [ ] Build content flywheel — consistent posting schedule
- [ ] Explore IndieAppSanta.com for periodic promotional bursts
- [ ] Consider web-only hard paywall funnel for Meta ads traffic
- [ ] Expand to additional paid channels based on learnings
